DHEERA Foundation

The Dheera Foundation was established in 2021 with an intention for betterment & uplift of society. Dheera Foundation was set up in Vizianagaram to promote Education among underprivileged children, Employment for youth & Healthcare for all needy in and around rural areas. Every year this foundation helps around 1 lakh children and their families. Their welfare projects are about Education, Employment & Healthcare was set up in urban areas and remote villages of Andhra Pradesh, India.

Dheera Foundation is interested in promoting and providing quality education, welfare of children and women, upliftment of poor, downtrodden and needy person's socially and economically by providing Health, Education & Employment.
